Hi! My name is Lindsey Howell


Welcome to my portfolio. I am a front-end developer that specializes in html, css, and javascript. I got started with programming through Harvard's cs50 course and have since built several applications. Here are some of my favorites. Please feel free to contact me if you have any questions, or would like to talk about business ventures.

ASMR Hub


For my final project for Harvard's cs50 course I created an online ASMR Hub to house all or most things associated with ASMR. I created several interactive components for a users viewing pleasure as well as pertinant information that would help guide anyone new to the idea of ASMR.

Northwest American Bulldog Breeding Website


I had a client who need a site to promote her and her husbands American Bulldog Breeding business. I created a website that showcased their current male and female breeding dogs and a way for an interested parties to get in contact with them to inquire about a particular puppy from a recent litter.
